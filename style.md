# Design Aesthetic: The Human Resonance of AI

## Mood & Atmosphere
- **Cinematic Brutalism**: High-contrast, grainy film textures, and a sense of raw, unpolished beauty.
- **Atmospheric Depth**: Use of layered gradients, blurs, and light leaks to create an immersive, almost dreamlike experience.
- **Human-Centric**: Despite being about AI, the focus is on human emotion, resonance, and the "craft" of interaction.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: Deep blacks and off-whites (paper-like textures).
- **Accents**: 
  - **Resonance (Skylar)**: Warm oranges, sunset glows, and amber light leaks.
  - **Observations (Describy)**: Electric teals, cool cyans, and neon-blue highlights.
- **Muted States**: Grayscale by default, transitioning to full color to signify "activation" or "human connection."

## Typography
- **Headings**: Noto Serif — even-stroked serif for titles, section headings, navbar, and the "THE CRAFT" watermark. Light weight for elegant section titles and nav; black weight for hero title and watermark.
- **Body & Labels**: Space Grotesk — geometric sans-serif for body text, labels, links, CTAs, and all functional text. Uppercase with wide tracking for labels and micro-copy.
- **Why these fonts**: Noto Serif's even strokes degrade gracefully under the hero's blur-reveal animation — high-contrast serifs (Bodoni, Playfair) lose their defining detail in blur. Space Grotesk's smooth geometry matches the site's slow, organic motion design.

## Motion & Interaction
- **Slow Drift**: Subtle, continuous movement in background elements to prevent static feel.
- **Slam-in / Fade-in**: Dramatic entry animations for key sections.
- **Fluid Transitions**: Smooth color shifts and scale transforms on hover/interaction.
- **Grain Overlay**: A persistent, subtle film grain overlay to unify all visual elements.

## Key Components
- **The Eye**: A central, immersive video element representing the "gaze" of the AI.
- **Bento Grids**: Structured but dynamic layouts for project modules.
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent surfaces with backdrop blurs for UI overlays.
